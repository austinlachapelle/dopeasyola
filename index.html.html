01 / The Story

From a trunk full of weed to a global brand.

Started off adulthood with a trunk full of weed and a route up and down California. Found Instagram. Found the cannabis community. Started recreating favorite movie scenes with miniatures, nugs, and small toys — and never looked back.

Eleven years later, Thomas Araujo is the first cannabis YouTuber to ever cross 2 million subscribers. Forbes called him the most popular cannabis content creator on Earth. He and co-host Marty O'Neill became the first podcast duo in history to give a TED Talk.

The secret isn't algorithmic — it's stubborn. Despite shadow bans, demonetization, and 30+ Instagram deletions, the audience kept coming back. Over six years he's personally answered 240,000+ comments. No bots. Four to six hours a day. Every day. Talking to fans.

Now he runs four brands, hosts a top-10 Spotify podcast, tours nationally as a stand-up — and still posts reviews from the same kitchen counter.
